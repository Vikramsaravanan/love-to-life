Love:


Every love is different, shaped by the unique hearts and stories of those involved—no two bonds are the same. And because of this richness, everybody deserves love: it’s a fundamental right of every soul. But love isn't a game—don’t play poorly with someone’s feelings; treat it with sincerity and respect. No one in this world is incapable of loving—love is innate, a universal power within us all. At its heart, love is to give—offering your time, kindness, care, and generosity selflessly. True love blooms not by taking or expecting, but by giving your full presence with an open and compassionate heart.
